Quick Rules

🌙 Night Phase
Everyone closes their eyes. The Mafia choose someone to eliminate. With 7+ players, the Doctor protects one person, and the Detective investigates one person.
☀️ Day Phase
The town debates and votes to eliminate a suspect. Majority wins. The eliminated player's role is revealed to all.
🏆 Winning
Citizens win by eliminating all Mafia.
Mafia wins when their numbers equal the remaining town.
📋 Rules
• You cannot vote for yourself
• Eliminated players must stay silent
• The Doctor cannot protect themselves
• Everyone must vote: no skipping
MAFIA
A game of trust and deception
A host device runs the game. Each player secretly views their role, then the game begins. Roles are revealed when players are eliminated.

Add Players

4 to 16 players

How to Play

🌙
1. The Night
Everyone closes their eyes. The host will call on the Mafia to wake up and secretly choose one player to eliminate.

If playing with 7+ people, the Doctor and Detective will also wake up to use their special abilities.
☀️
2. The Day
Everyone opens their eyes. The host announces who (if anyone) was eliminated during the night.

The survivors then have a few minutes to debate, accuse each other, and try to figure out who the Mafia members are.
🗳️
3. The Vote
Debate ends and the town votes. The player with the most votes is eliminated and their role is revealed.

The cycle of Night and Day repeats until either the Citizens catch all the Mafia, or the Mafia outnumbers the Citizens.
